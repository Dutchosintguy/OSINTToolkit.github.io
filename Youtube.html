Youtube:

In order to use some of the tools, you must know the channel name of the subject(s) you wish to research. To identify a Youtube channel name you must load the Youtube Profile of your subject and right click the page before selecting View Page Source from the options list. While on the Source Page, press CTRL+F and search for the phrase: "channelId" content=", the subjects channel name will be after this phrase.
