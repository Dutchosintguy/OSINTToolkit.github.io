Google+ & LinkedIn:

Google+

In order to use some of the tools, you must know the Google+ Account ID of the subject(s) you wish to research.

To identify an Account ID you must load the Google+ Profile of your subject and right click the page. From there a list of options will appear, select View Page Source from the list. While on the Source Page, press CTRL+F and search for the phrase data-oid=, the subjects Account ID will be the number after this phrase.
